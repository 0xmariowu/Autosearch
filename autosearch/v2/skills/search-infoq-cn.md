# Platform

InfoQ China (InfoQ 中国) — Chinese edition of InfoQ, focused on enterprise software development practices. Strong in architecture, DevOps, AI/ML adoption at scale, and technology leadership content.

# When To Choose It

Choose this when:

- need enterprise-grade technical case studies from Chinese companies
- searching for architecture decisions and patterns at scale
- looking for technology adoption stories from large organizations
- the topic involves production-level engineering practices

# How To Search

- `site:infoq.cn {Chinese keywords}`

Example queries:
- `site:infoq.cn AI agent 架构 企业实践`
- `site:infoq.cn LLM 工程化 生产环境`
- `site:infoq.cn 自进化系统 设计模式`

# Standard Output Schema

- `source`: `"infoq-cn"`

# Date Metadata

InfoQ articles have publication dates. Extract from snippet.

# Quality Bar

This skill is working when it discovers enterprise-level Chinese technology practices and case studies not found in English media.
