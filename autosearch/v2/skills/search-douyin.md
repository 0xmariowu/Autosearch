# Platform

Douyin (抖音) — China's TikTok. Short-form videos including tech demos, product showcases, quick tutorials.

# When To Choose It

Choose this when:

- searching for viral tech demos in Chinese market
- need quick product showcases and reviews
- looking for trending tech content in China

# How To Search

- `site:douyin.com {Chinese keywords}` — limited results due to app-first design
- Better: `WebSearch "抖音 {keywords}"` to find content shared/discussed on other platforms

- Parse share links for video metadata
- Watermark-free content access
- Creator and engagement data

# Standard Output Schema

- `source`: `"douyin"`

# Date Metadata

Extract upload dates from metadata when available.

# Quality Bar

This skill is working when it discovers viral Chinese tech content and demos not found through text-based searches.
