## When to use

Use when the query needs broad web coverage and there is no stronger platform-specific channel.

## Quality signals

- Relevant titles with strong entity overlap
- Trusted domains and official documentation
- Snippets that contain the target terms or constraints

## Known limits

- Result quality depends on DuckDuckGo coverage
- Snippets can be sparse or noisy
- Upstream throttling can reduce reliability
