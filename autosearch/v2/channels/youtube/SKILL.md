## When to use

Use when the best evidence is likely to be in videos, including walkthroughs, conference talks, product demos, interviews, and tutorial content.

## Quality signals

- Relevant title and creator name
- Usable description text and video length
- Multiple matching uploads across credible channels

## Known limits

- The parser depends on YouTube page structure and continuation tokens
- Some videos have sparse descriptions
- Search behavior can vary by region and anti-bot controls
