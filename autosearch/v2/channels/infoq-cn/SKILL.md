## When to use

Use when the query involves architecture, distributed systems, cloud platforms, or Chinese-language enterprise engineering reporting commonly covered by InfoQ China.

## Quality signals

- Strong overlap with architecture or platform terminology
- Recognizable speakers, companies, or technical programs
- Snippets that point to concrete engineering discussion

## Known limits

- Results depend on Baidu indexing quality for InfoQ China
- Older conference and news coverage can outrank fresher deep dives
- Snippets may underrepresent long-form article nuance
