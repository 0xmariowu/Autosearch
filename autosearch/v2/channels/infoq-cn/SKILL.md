## Content types

Chinese enterprise-tech articles, architecture writeups, platform engineering coverage, conference summaries, and technical reporting from InfoQ China. The content is more editorial and enterprise-oriented than generic developer blogs.

## Language

Chinese only. It works best with Chinese enterprise and infrastructure terminology.

## Best for

Chinese enterprise architecture patterns, middleware, distributed systems, microservices, and DevOps discussions in a Chinese context. It is strongest when the topic sits in mainstream enterprise engineering rather than consumer app development.

## Blind spots

Scope is relatively narrow and enterprise-focused. It is not the right source for lightweight tutorials, consumer product discussion, or broad web coverage, and it will miss many niche open-source implementation details.

## Quality signals

Editorial curation is the main advantage here. Recognizable companies, concrete architecture terms, and article framing that points to implementation or scale problems are better signals than generic trend pieces.
