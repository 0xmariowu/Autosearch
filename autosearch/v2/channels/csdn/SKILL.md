## When to use

Use when the query is about Chinese-language developer tutorials, framework usage, debugging notes, or code-centric blog posts commonly published on CSDN.

## Quality signals

- Clear code, framework, or error-message overlap in the title
- Snippets that reference implementation steps or concrete fixes
- Recent posts for fast-moving tools or platform changes

## Known limits

- Results depend on Baidu indexing quality for CSDN
- Some pages are shallow reposts or SEO-heavy summaries
- Snippets can be noisy when the source page is cluttered
