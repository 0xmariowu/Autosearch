## When to use

Use when the best evidence is in Hacker News stories or community discussion around developer tools, launches, or industry news.

## Quality signals

- High point counts and active discussion
- Story titles tightly aligned with the query
- Recent submission timestamps for current topics

## Known limits

- Limited to story hits from the Algolia index
- Snippets are synthetic score summaries rather than full text
- Discussion quality varies widely by topic
