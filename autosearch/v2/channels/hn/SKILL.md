## Content types

Hacker News story submissions: linked articles, launch posts, Show HN items, and their associated community score and comment activity. It captures what the HN community noticed and argued about, not the full web.

## Language

English only in practice. Queries should be phrased in English and usually around developer or startup terminology.

## Best for

Developer opinion on tools and products, launch reactions, technical debates, and community temperature checks around industry news. It is especially useful when you care what a technical audience thought about something.

## Blind spots

HN has a strong US and tech-bubble bias, weak coverage of non-English sources, and poor reach into academic or mainstream consumer topics. It is also not a substitute for primary documentation or rigorous evidence.

## Quality signals

Points are the clearest community-validation signal. High comment counts, relevant titles, and submissions from official sources or recognized authors usually indicate a stronger result than a low-engagement link dump.
