## When to use

Use when you want startup launches, product discovery pages, maker commentary, or Product Hunt listings for software and tools.

## Quality signals

- Result titles align with the product or category you want
- Snippets indicate launch pages, collections, or maker context
- Product names are specific enough to disambiguate similar tools

## Known limits

- Results depend on DuckDuckGo indexing quality for Product Hunt pages
- Snippets may not include launch metrics or maker comments
- Older launches can outrank newer but more relevant products
