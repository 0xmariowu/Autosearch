## When to use

Use when you want professional profiles, company pages, hiring signals, executive identities, or business posts that are likely indexed from LinkedIn.

## Quality signals

- Titles clearly identify a person, company, role, or job page
- Snippets include professional keywords such as industry, headcount, or title
- Result URLs imply the expected LinkedIn page type

## Known limits

- Results depend on DuckDuckGo indexing quality for LinkedIn pages
- Many LinkedIn pages expose limited public snippet text
- Search can surface stale jobs or partial profile mirrors
