## When to use

Use when you want startup profiles, funding history, investor pages, acquisitions, or company metadata that is likely indexed from Crunchbase.

## Quality signals

- Company or investor names match the query directly
- Titles indicate funding rounds, acquisitions, or organization profiles
- Result snippets include stage, sector, or location context

## Known limits

- Results depend on DuckDuckGo indexing quality for Crunchbase pages
- Many Crunchbase pages expose limited snippet text in search results
- Coverage can miss newer or less prominent companies
