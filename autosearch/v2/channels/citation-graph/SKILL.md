## When to use

Use when the query refers to a known paper and the goal is to find downstream citing work.

## Quality signals

- The seed paper resolves correctly
- Citing papers have clear titles, authors, and years
- Citation counts help prioritize influential follow-on work

## Known limits

- Results depend on correctly matching the initial paper
- Coverage is limited to Semantic Scholar graph data
- Rate limiting can reduce reliability
