## When to use

- Breaking tech news and announcements (researchers, companies post here first)
- Real-time opinions on tools, frameworks, papers
- Threads with deep technical analysis
- Product launches and demos

## Quality signals

- Verified accounts, high engagement (likes/retweets)
- Threads (longer content, more substance)
- Links to papers, repos, blog posts in tweets

## Known limits

- Search via ddgs site: only returns indexed tweets (not real-time)
- No access to replies/threads structure
- Some tweets are behind login walls
- Both twitter.com and x.com domains searched for coverage
