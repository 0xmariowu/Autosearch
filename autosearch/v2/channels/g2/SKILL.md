## When to use

Use when you want software review pages, category comparisons, buyer-intent research, or vendor listings from G2.

## Quality signals

- Result titles indicate category pages, reviews, or product profiles
- Query terms match the software name, use case, or buyer category
- Snippets suggest comparative or review-oriented context

## Known limits

- Results depend on DuckDuckGo indexing quality for G2 pages
- Search snippets often omit rating breakdowns and review depth
- Vendor SEO pages can outrank direct review pages
