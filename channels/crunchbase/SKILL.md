## Content types

Structured company profiles, funding records, investor pages, acquisitions, and startup metadata from Crunchbase. This is company data, not commentary or technical analysis.

## Language

English. Company names are universal, but the surrounding metadata and search behavior are English-oriented.

## Best for

Startup funding lookup, investor discovery, competitive landscape mapping, and quick company metadata checks. It is useful when the answer should look like structured business facts rather than articles or opinions.

## Blind spots

It has no technical depth, no product evaluation, and no real user sentiment. If you want how a product works or what engineers think of it, this channel is the wrong tool.

## Quality signals

The data is structured and generally reliable. Exact company-name match, clear funding-stage references, and investor or acquisition metadata are the strongest signals that the result is the right entity.
