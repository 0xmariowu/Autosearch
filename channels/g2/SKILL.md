## Content types

Software review pages, category rankings, user-rating summaries, and product profiles from G2. It is a review aggregation channel aimed mostly at software buyers.

## Language

English. The platform and review ecosystem are overwhelmingly English-language and enterprise-oriented.

## Best for

Enterprise tool comparisons, user satisfaction checks, and quick feature-matrix style buying research. It is useful when the question is "how is this vendor perceived in the software-buying market."

## Blind spots

G2 has a strong enterprise bias, weak coverage of open-source tools, and limited technical depth. It is a bad source for implementation guidance, code-level evaluation, or community hobbyist tools.

## Quality signals

Review count and rating are the main signals. Category relevance matters too: a highly rated product in the wrong category is less useful than a solid product with many reviews in the exact category you care about.
