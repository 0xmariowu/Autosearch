## Content types

WeChat public account articles, long-form Chinese essays, company posts, commentary, and curated industry analysis. It often surfaces content that never appears cleanly on the open web.

## Language

Chinese only. This channel is not useful for English search except for brand names that appear inside Chinese articles.

## Best for

Chinese industry analysis, company announcements distributed via WeChat, and long-form Chinese commentary that is unique to the WeChat ecosystem. It is valuable when the content you want is likely trapped inside the WeChat publishing world.

## Blind spots

It is partially walled off, indexing is inconsistent, and many good articles are hard to surface reliably. Results can also be stale, missing, or skewed by Sogou availability and platform constraints.

## Quality signals

Readable snippets, recognizable account names, and publish dates help. Read count would be useful but usually is not available in results, so source reputation matters more than ranking position.
