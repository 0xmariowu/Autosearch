## Content types

Product launch pages, maker blurbs, category listings, and product descriptions from Product Hunt. The content is lightweight and launch-oriented rather than deep or technical.

## Language

English. Product names are often enough, but the platform context is English-speaking startup culture.

## Best for

Discovering new tools and products, checking how a product positioned itself at launch, and getting a quick market-facing summary. It is a discovery channel, not an evaluation channel.

## Blind spots

There is almost no technical depth, and launch copy is usually marketing-heavy. You will not get implementation detail, long-term product quality, or serious comparison data from Product Hunt alone.

## Quality signals

Upvote count is the clearest social signal. Specific product descriptions, launch timing, and whether the result is the direct product page rather than a list page help separate useful hits from noise.
