## Content types

ML models and datasets from HuggingFace Hub. Models include pipeline tags (text-generation, image-classification, etc.) and download counts. Datasets include download counts and descriptions.

## Language

Primarily English. Model and dataset names are typically English, but some Chinese-language models exist (search with Chinese keywords works).

## Best for

Finding specific ML models, pre-trained weights, datasets for fine-tuning, and trending AI resources. Especially useful for AI/ML topics where the user needs actual implementations rather than articles about them.

## Blind spots

No paper abstracts or research context. Model cards vary wildly in quality. Download count is a popularity signal, not a quality signal. Does not cover non-ML software or general code repositories.

## Quality signals

Download count is the primary ranking signal. Models with pipeline tags are generally more usable. Datasets with high download counts are more likely to be well-documented.
