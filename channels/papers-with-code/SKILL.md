## Content types

Recent ML/AI research papers from HuggingFace Daily Papers (which absorbed Papers with Code). Each result includes title, abstract, authors, publication date, and community upvotes.

## Language

Primarily English. Academic papers are overwhelmingly in English regardless of author origin.

## Best for

Finding trending and recent AI research papers, especially those with community attention (upvotes). Complements arXiv by surfacing papers that the community finds interesting, not just the latest submissions.

## Blind spots

Biased toward recent and popular papers. Does not cover older or less trendy research. The daily papers feed may miss niche or specialized work. No direct link to code repositories (unlike the original Papers with Code).

## Quality signals

Upvote count indicates community interest. Author count and affiliation are in metadata. Publication date helps assess recency. Abstract quality varies.
